About Us

iReTron is the easiest, fastest and greenest reCommerce service to help you trade in your used electronics. Whether you want to trade in your iPhone, iPad, Kindle or other electronics, iReTron's got you covered.

What can I sell to iReTron?

You can sell your used iPhone, iPad, iPod or other used phones to iReTron. We also purchase ebook readers, tablets and calculators, so sell your used electronics to us today.

How does it work?

Send us your item in a box with our prepaid shipping label. Once received, your used mobile phones or other used electronics are inspected to make sure their condition matches your evaluation. We will send you payment within 5 business days of receiving your box.

Safe and Easy

We remove all data from each item we receive to ensure your complete privacy and peace of mind. We make the experience easy by helping with shipping and sending you updates at every step.
