How it works

Simple steps to sell your devices

Find

  • Type your device's brand or model number into the search bar on the homepage or search in the many categories of electronics we accept. If you can't find your product in our catalog, please tell us so we can add it, or we can offer you a personal quote.
  • Let us know about the condition of your device and the accessories included, and we will give you an instant quote.
  • If you accept, click Add item to shipping package to add the device to your selling list. Similar to Add to cart.
  • Click Checkout Now when you are done entering your devices to the list.
  • Then you will be asked to Please Login or Register. First time sellers chose Register,.
  • Enter contact information and choose your preferred payment method and click Register.
  • Click Finalize Package.

Ship:

At the end of the transaction, we will email you a pre-paid shipping label as long as something in your package has value. Carefully package the items and apply the pre-paid shipping label to your package. Drop your box off at any USPS Office. After you send your box, sit back, relax and let us do the work.

Get Paid:

When your items arrive, we will check that conditions of the device match your submission and send your payment via check or PayPal. All personal data and information on your devices will be deleted as a part of our service. If anything drastically differs from your original submission, we will email an explanation of what is wrong, and give you a new quote. If you don't like our new offer, we will send your devices back for free.
